hackEDU is a nonprofit organization that helps high school students start and lead programming clubs at their schools.

What We Do

Resources

hackEDU provides open-ended curriculum, mentors, and any resources needed to students starting and running clubs.

Community

We care about building a global community of students creating peer-to-peer learning environments.

Open Source

We're a new type of startup. Everything from our website to our financials is open source under a permissive license.
